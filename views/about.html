Shade Controller was created by Joseph Adams.

For more information or to contact the author, please visit www.techministry.blog.

This program uses DCTTECH USB Relays which can be purchased online. They come in a variety of models. A 2-relay model is needed to operate both the up and down functionality.

The program operates on TCP Port 4400.

To activate Relay 1 (Shades Up): 
                http://ipaddress:4400/shades_up
To activate Relay 2 (Shades Down): 
                http://ipaddress:4400/shades_down
